Ingredients


  • 3 (medium/large) ripe bananas mashed with a fork

  • ½ cup unsalted butter (8 Tbsp) at room temperature

  • 3/4 cup white sugar

  • 2 eggs lightly beaten

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

  • 1 tsp baking soda

  • ½ tsp salt

  • ½ tsp vanilla

  • 1 cup walnuts

  • ½ cup raisins
